## 🎬 Animation Effects

- **Sidebar Slide**: 300ms ease transition
- **Card Hover**: Scale + shadow on hover
- **Button Press**: Subtle scale down
- **Page Transitions**: Smooth route changes
- **Dropdown**: Slide and fade in
- **Toast Notifications**: Slide up from bottom
